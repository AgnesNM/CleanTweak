The Story Behind....

Over the last 4 years of doing digital marketing, we noticed a trend among our clients. They were not able to achieve their marketing goals, yet they kept creating new content, and pushing it. In fact, one of them had already published over 10,000 pieces of content. What really was the problem? What could we really do about it? For that client, we repackaged several pieces of content. To our surprise, this content was even outdoing the newly created one. The question lingered though...what would be a lasting solution?
