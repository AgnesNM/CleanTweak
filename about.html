The Story Behind....

Over the last 4 years of doing digital marketing, we noticed a trend among our clients. They were not able to achieve their marketing goals, yet they kept creating new content, and pushing it. In fact, one of them had already published over 10,000 pieces of content. What really was the problem? What could we really do about it? For that client, we repackaged several pieces of content. To our surprise, this content was even outdoing the newly created one. The question lingered though...what would be a lasting solution?

What Next?

The Idea...

How about something that can collate content? What would that be? It needs to be some software, in order to actually make marketing easier.

Prototype...

So we started building CleanTweak, a digital marketing tool that is able to collate a brand's content that was published in a particular period of time.

Testing...

We are looking to pitch this awesome tool at the oncoming East Africa Com on 14th to 15th May.

Our Mission

We are looking to make it easier for marketing teams to do marketing, by saving the time used to create new content, as well as cut down the cost of creating content.

Goes without saying that repurposing content is cheaper when compared to creating new content.

Why CleanTweak?

  • Accurate and relevant brand content
  • Effective and efficient digital marketing
  • Intuitive product usage

CleanTweak is a digital marketing tool that collates all your 'old'published content into one place, allowing your marketing team to repackage it the way that best suits your brand.
